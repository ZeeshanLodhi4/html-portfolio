Zeeshan's Cold Coffee Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 2 Tablespoons Of Instant Coffee Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Of Powdered Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Of Warm Water
  • 1 Cup Of Chilled Milk
  • 4-5 Ice Cubes
  • Whipped Cream Or Chocolate Syrup For Topping (Optional)

Instructions :

  1. In a tiny bowl , add instant coffee powder , powdered sugar and warm water.
  2. Whisk it up by using an egg whisk or a small strainer until the mixture gets foamy.
  3. Now , grab a cup and add the mixture into it.
  4. In the same cup , add the chilled milk and mix everything up and on top of that add ice cubes.
  5. Finally , add whipped cream or chocolate syrup on top , whichever you prefer
  6. Ta Da! Your Perfectly Chilled Coffee Is Ready To Serve.
