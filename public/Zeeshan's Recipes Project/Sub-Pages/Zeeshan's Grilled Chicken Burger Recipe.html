Zeeshan's Grilled Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients :

  • Burger Bun
  • ¼ Chicken Boneless Breast (fillet)
  • Salt, pepper, paprika powder and vinegar/lemon juice (seasoning)
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 rings of onion
  • Chopped lettuce
  • 3 thin slices of cucumber
  • Mayonoise
  • ½ teaspoon of mustard

Instructions :

  1. In a bowl, add the chicken fillet and to it add salt, pepper, paprika powder and lil bit of vinegar/lemon juice and mix it up.
  2. Heat a pan , add some oil and cook ur chicken fillet well on both sides.
  3. When both sides of fillet are cooked, smash the fillet with the help of a masher and let it cook more on both sides.
  4. Then turn off the heat and add the slice of cheese on top and put it aside.
  5. For the sauce, in a tiny bowl, add mayonoise , salt, pepper, vinegar/lemon juice and mustard and mix it up.
  6. On a plate, place the lower bun and add the sauce on top and then add lettuce, slices of tomato and cucmber.
  7. Then on top, place the cooked fillet and add onion.
  8. Finally, on the upper bun, add the sauce and place it on top and Ta-Da! Your delicious grilled chicken burger is ready to serve.
