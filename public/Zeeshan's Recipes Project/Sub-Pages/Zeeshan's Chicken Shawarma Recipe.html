Zeeshan's Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 1 tortilla wrap
  • 200 grams of boneless chicken
  • 4-5 slices of cucmber
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Salt , pepper , paprike and vinegar/lemon juice (seasoning)
  • Mayonoise

Instructions :

  1. In a bowl, add chicken and add salt, pepper, paprike and vinegar/lemon juice to it and miz it up.
  2. Then cook the chicken in a pan until its well done.Take it off and mash it up a bit.
  3. For the Sauce, in a tiny bowl add mayonoise and to it add salt, pepper, vinegar/lemon juice and minced garlic and mix it up.
  4. Place tortilla wrap on a plate and spread the sauce on it.
  5. Then on top of that, place the cucumber slices and the mashed chicken and some more sauce.
  6. Then roll the tortilla and heat it up on a pan until its golden brown.
  7. Take it off the heat and Ta-Da! Your delicious and mouthwatering chicken shawarma is ready to serve.
