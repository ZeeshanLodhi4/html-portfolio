Zeeshan's Chicken Club Sandwhich Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 3 slices of bread (of your choice)
  • 100 grams of boneless chicken (sliced)
  • Chopped lettuce
  • 2-3 slices of tomato
  • 4-5 slices of cucumber
  • Mayonoise
  • Salt, pepper and vinegar/lemon juice (seasoning)

Instructions :

  1. In a bowl, add chicken then add salt, pepper and vinegar or lemon juice to the chicken and mix it up.
  2. Heat up a pan, add oil and cook your chicken until done.
  3. For the sauce, in a tiny bowl, add mayonoise , a pinch of salt, black pepper and lil bit of vinegar/lemon juice.
  4. Take a slice of bread, put some sauce and spread and put lettuce on it.
  5. Take half of your cooked chicken and put on it then place other slice of bread on top followed by some more sauce spread on it.
  6. Then put slices of tomato and cucumbers on it and on top of that add the remaining chicken.
  7. Now place the last slice of bread on top.
  8. On a pan , toast the sandwhich on each side until a nice golden brown color comes.
  9. Take it off the heat and Ta-Da! Your delicious and scrumptious chicken club sandwhich is ready to serve.
